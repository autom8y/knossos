The framework itself -- a context-engineering meta-framework for Claude Code. Named after the Minoan labyrinth at Knossos. The `.knossos/` directory contains framework configuration, rite definitions, and templates. The ari binary is its CLI, named after Ariadne who provided the clew to navigate the maze.
