The per-rite entry agent and coordinator, derived from the Linear B inscription on tablet KN Gg 702: da-pu2-ri-to-jo po-ti-ni-ja ("Potnia of the Labyrinth"). Each rite has its own Potnia who serves as the presiding authority within that rite's domain — clear guidance, not cryptic prophecy. Potnia coordinates the specialist agents within her rite, routes work to the appropriate specialist, and ensures the rite's workflow is followed. Distinguished from Pythia (the cross-rite oracle consulted before entering the labyrinth): Potnia presides within, Pythia advises from without.
