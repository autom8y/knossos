The provenance owner trichotomy (knossos, satellite, rite) treated as a hospitality contract. Named for the Greek concept of xenia, the sacred obligation between host and guest. In Knossos, the platform (host) guarantees that user content (guest) is never destroyed during sync. Three owners: knossos-owned regions are overwritten on sync, satellite-owned regions are preserved, and rite content follows manifest declarations. Violations of xenia — deleting user files, overwriting satellite regions, or ignoring provenance — are architectural sins, not just bugs.
