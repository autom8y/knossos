# Moonshot Plan Template

> Long-term architectural vision with scenario planning, migration phases, and investment analysis.

{The future we're planning for and why it matters}

## Quality Gate

**Moonshot Plan complete when:**
- Scenarios include probability and trigger signals
- Migration phases have reversibility assessment
- Investment summary covers all phases
- Decision points have timing triggers, not just dates
